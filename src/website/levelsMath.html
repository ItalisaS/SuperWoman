Math

The category Math has the aim to solve arithmetic problems.
At the beginning of a level you will see a equation and during the level you will have to collect the fitting numbers to solve the equation.
You need to find the correct numbers until the time has run out. If you win, the next level will be unlocked - if not, you'll have to replay the level.
Good luck and have fun!
