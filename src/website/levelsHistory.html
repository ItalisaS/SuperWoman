History

The category History has the aim to find the correct word to a shown picture or question.
At the beginning of a level you will either see a picture of a historical person or you will get a question which you will have to answer. During the level you will have to collect the fitting symbols to find the corresponding name or numbers.
You need to find the correct symbols until the time has run out. If you win, the next level will be unlocked - if not, you'll have to replay the level.
Good luck and have fun!
