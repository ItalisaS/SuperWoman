Word Hunting

The category Word Hunting has the aim to find the correct word to a shown picture.
At the beginning of a level you will see a picture of an item and during the level you will have to collect the fitting letters to complete the word. You need to find the correct letters until the time has run out.
If you win, the next level will be unlocked - if not, you'll have to replay the level.

Good luck and have fun!
