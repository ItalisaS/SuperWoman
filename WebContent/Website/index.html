Welcome to SuperWoman!

Here you can play a fantastic and fun game - we hope you'll like it!

Our game is more than just a normal fun game - while playing it you can also improve your mathematical abilities and your power of concentration. We have three different categories: WordHunting, Maths and History. Each category has a collection of some levels, but in the beginning only one level is unlocked - the second one will be unlocked after finishing the previous one. To make it even more fun you can compete against your friends - on a highscore you can see the 10 best players!

Have fun playing our game!
